Features

Everything your team needs

Built for Weee!'s multicultural campaigns, not generic influencer marketing.

🌏
Ethnic Market Filters
Filter by Korean, Chinese, Mexican, Indian, and Japanese markets—each with cultural insights, key foods, and trending content.
📊
Creator stats at a glance
Every creator card shows followers, engagement rate, average views, and estimated cost — so you can evaluate fit and budget without leaving the tool.
✦
AI campaign matcher
Describe your campaign in plain English and the AI recommends 2–3 creators with specific reasoning around cultural fit, food content alignment, and seasonal timing.
✉
Personalized outreach drafts
Generate a tailored DM for any creator in one click — referencing their actual content, cultural background, and campaign fit. Sounds human, not templated.
🗄
Auto-syncs to Airtable
Every creator you shortlist is automatically saved to your Airtable "Creators" base with their name, handle, platform, and status. When you copy an outreach message, the message is saved and status updates to "Contacted" — keeping your whole team in sync without any manual data entry.
